Get to know the members of group 19!

Group 19 consists of 4 friends who all study computer science at Howest in Bruges. For our studies we have to follow an international project. We originally planned to do our international project in Sweden, but due to the pandemic that event was cancelled. So we had to come up with our own project: A cybersecurity CTF event.
